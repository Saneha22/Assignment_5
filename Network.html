Introduction to Networks

Computer network is an interconnection of various computers to share software, hardware and data through a communication medium between them. There is no limit to the distance between two computers in network. Technically defining, a computer network is an interconnected collection of autonomous computers

Need for networking

  1. To share computers files:

    2. Networks enable users to share files with others.

  2. To reduce cost of data transfer :

    3. The cost of transfer of documents using computers connected on networks is cheaper than other conventional means like telegrams.

  3. Verify data transfers :

    4. Fluctuations of prices in foreign exchange and shares can be communicated instanataneously using the medium of computer communications.

  4. High reliability :

    5. All files could be replicated on two or three machines, so if one of them is unavailable, the other copies could be used

What is Internet ?

The term internet has been coined from two terms. Inter connection and Net work. A network is simply a group of computers that are connected together for sharing information and resources.


Thus, Internet is a network of networks that are:

  1. Interconnected physcially
  2. of communicating and sharing data with each other.
  3. Able to act together as a single network
